Details

Why should you choose our shuttle service?

running-wolves is a dynamic company and your reliable mobility service provider.
Our own vehicle fleet is available all over Germany.
The company was founded by Hans Sebald, who has more than 20 years of experience in the tourist field. Since 2002, he has concentrated on the area of passenger service.
Business (and Private) travellers (as well as travel agencies), agencies, travel and also private persons trust our service and our efficiency day after day.
Our goal is to be the first choice of our customers. To achieve this, we offer constantly high-grade service standards and a very fair and open business philosophy.
Features

Features

Discover the exceptional features of our limousine shuttle service

Shuttle Service

For groups of all size we offer individual services and flexible quotations with shuttle vans or luxury class busses

Airport Service

Door-to-terminal service and vice versa. We are informed of the current flight schedule at all times. Including luggage assistance and individual route planning.

Transfer to Trade Fairs

Concentrate entirely on your trade fair visit, we take care of everything else. Comfortable and punctual collection from the airport or your hotel. No annoying search for parking space.

Chauffeur Service

Our trained chauffeurs will bring you to your appointments comfortably and safely. However, they can do even more: Accept calls or run errands. They are familiar with the best restaurants and also will be glad to be your personal sightseeing guide. And of course always in conformity with your desired dress code.

Sightseeing

Our sightseeing programmes are customized according to your wishes. Enjoy your special personal tour in comfortable vehicles. The multi-lingual chauffeur is your guide and offers you and your guests a unique view on culture and city life.

Transfer to/from the harbour

We will bring you to any German seaport comfortably and safely. With our service your rest and relaxation already begins on land.

Our Fleet

Experience the elegance and comfort of our limousines Our entire fleet exclusively consists of new vehicles. Selection of vehicle quality and maximum comfort have top priority. Our limousines and vans all have superior equipment. Our limousine fleet comprises various vehicles of the upper class, for instance Skoda Superb.
But other car models can be rented for the transfer on your request.

Depending on your requirement and individual request, we provide prestigious vans and mini busses, for example VW Touran with 3 seats or VW Caravelle Comfortline with 8 seats. The extended wheel base always guarantees sufficient storage space for your luggage. In cooperation with responsible and reliable partners we also offer you large busses.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive.
What services does your limousine shuttle company offer? Wir bieten professionelle Transportdienstleistungen für verschiedene Anlässe wie Flughafentransfers, Firmenveranstaltungen, Hochzeiten und vieles mehr.
What kind of vehicles are available for booking? Our modern fleet consists exclusively of new, high quality luxury vehicles such as the Skoda Superb. Each of our vehicles offers maximum comfort and upmarket equipment. Other luxury models are also available for individual hire if required.
How do I make a reservation? You can easily make a reservation by contacting our customer service.
Are your drivers licensed and experienced? Yes, all our drivers are professionals with experience in chauffeur services.
What safety precautions have you taken? Our actions are focused on the well-being and safety of our customers. We only use new, approved vehicles with short service intervals. Our chauffeurs undergo thorough basic training, regular driver training and adhere to working time restrictions to ensure they remain focused and safe at all times.
